The Cal Archery Team is a group of elite and passionate
that compete in tournaments at the local, regional, and
national level. We travel to various competitions
throughout California and the US, shooting at tournaments
like State Indoor, National Indoor, Western Outdoor
Regionals, and National Outdoor just to name a few!

Shooting at competitions is not only fun,
but is also a way to apply all of your
practice to show some results! These
competitions are excellent opportunities
to better yourself, as well as connect more
with your teammates. Archers of all levels
are welcome to shoot at our competitions!

Being on team also gives you access
to team practices! Team practices are
every Saturday from 9 am - 1 pm, unless
otherwise stated. This practice is mainly
centered on training for competitions
through technique exercises, drills, and
the help of our experienced members
and coaches

How To Join
Team

Eligibility for joining team is based on two conditions.

The first condition is frequent presence and activity at
club practices and events. By joining team you are
expected to help teach at Ascent, help with beginner
sessions, work Golden Bear Open, and help with any
other relevant club activities.

The second condition is being able to shoot the
minimum team score for your division. Team tryout
dates are listed on the below, and team members are
expected to make their respective scores on that day.
Otherwise official scoring can be remade at official
scoring at a team practice.

Division Indoor Outdoor
Recurve 430/600 430/720
Barebow 350/600 340/720
Bowhunter 500/600 540/720
Compound 500/600 560/720
