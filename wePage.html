Club Hours

We welcome archers of all levels! Come to a club
practice to learn how to shoot, free shoot, work on
technique, or simply hang out with friends! Club
practice hours are open hours, so you may come
and go as you need.

Cal Archery Is a Sports Club run through
Rec Sports. In order to become a member
you must have an active RSF membership
with the University. Our club also requires
the payment of club dues and signing up
onto the Cal Archery roster.
